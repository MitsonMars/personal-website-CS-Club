What is butter chicken?

Butter Chicken is a dish that originates from New Dehi, India. It a tomato and butter based gravy that is known for its rich texture and mild spice.

A Popular Recipe

Ingredients: Chicken breast, Cilantro, Garlic, Butter, Heavy Cream, Ginger, Tomato Sauce, Oil, Garam Masala

1. Heat vegatable oil and melt 1 tbs of butter. Add diced chicken breast and cook until golden brown

2. Add garlic, ginger, spices and salt and cook for one minute

3. Add the passata

4. Simmer on medium heat for 5-10 minutes

5. Open lid and stir in Heavy Cream and cilantro. It's now ready to eat.

.

Served warm commonly with naan or rice!!!
